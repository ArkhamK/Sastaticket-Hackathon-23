Tourist spots

  • The Pearl-Qatar

    A luxurious man-made island offering upscale shopping, waterfront promenades, and exquisite dining options.

    Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of this traditional market, where you can wander through winding alleyways, shop for traditional handicrafts, and savor authentic Qatari cuisine.

    Experience Qatar's rich cultural heritage through art exhibitions, live performances, and cultural festivals held at this vibrant cultural hub.

Visa Procedure

Steps:

1. A tourist visa requires a passport, a recent photograph, and a visa application form.

2. Hold the following documents:

2.1. Passport: Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the date of your planned trip.

2.2. Visa application form: You can download the visa application form from the website of the Qatar embassy or consulate in Pakistan.

2.2. Recent photograph: A recent passport-sized photograph is required.

2.2. Visa fee: The visa fee varies depending on the type of visa.

3. Proof of accommodation: You must provide proof of accommodation in Qatar, such as a hotel reservation or a letter from your host.

4. Proof of funds: You must provide proof that you have sufficient funds to cover your stay in Qatar

5. Medical certificate: A medical certificate may be required if you are applying for a work visa.

6. The immigration officer will process your application and issue you a visa on arrival.

Additional things to keep in mind:

1. You may be asked to provide additional information or documents, such as a letter of invitation or a hotel reservation.

2. The immigration officer may have the discretion to deny your visa application.

3. It is always advisable to check with the Dominican embassy or consulate in your country before traveling to confirm the latest visa requirements.
